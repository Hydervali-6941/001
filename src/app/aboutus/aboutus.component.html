ABOUT
US

Voyage International Varsity of aviation and logistics was established in the year 2019 and is one of India's fastest growing academy in the field of aviation, hospitality and logistics management. The academy is specialised in providing students with best in class education and job oriented skill training to mould youths into certified professionals based on their field of choice. Voyage is a STED council approved academy which also provides a hundred percent job guarantee after every course. The academy allows students to be trained and groomed under the guidance of best-in-class faculty and industry experts. Our students have been placed at a number of reputed companies both in India and abroad.
